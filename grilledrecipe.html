Recipe 1: Classic Grilled Chicken

A simple and flavorful grilled chicken recipe that is easy to prepare.

Ingredients

  • 4 chicken breasts
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt, pepper, paprika, and garlic powder to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat the grill to medium heat.
  2. Rub the chicken with olive oil, salt, pepper, garlic powder, and paprika.
  3. Grill for 6-8 minutes per side or until fully cooked.
  4. Let rest for 5 minutes before serving.

Recipe 2: Spicy Grilled Chicken

Add a little heat with this spicy grilled chicken recipe.

Ingredients

  • 4 chicken thighs
  • 2 tbsp hot sauce
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt, cayenne pepper, and garlic powder to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat the grill to medium-high heat.
  2. Mix hot sauce, olive oil, salt, garlic powder, and cayenne pepper.
  3. Coat the chicken thighs in the mixture.
  4. Grill for 5-7 minutes per side until cooked through.
  5. Serve hot with extra hot sauce if desired.
