Recipe 1: Chicken Noodle Soup

A comforting chicken noodle soup for any season.

  • 1 lb chicken breast, diced
  • 4 cups chicken broth
  • 2 cups noodles
  • 1 carrot, sliced
  • 1 celery stalk, sliced
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. In a pot, bring chicken broth to a boil.
  2. Add chicken, carrot, and celery. Simmer for 10 minutes.
  3. Add noodles and cook until tender.
  4. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  5. Serve hot.

Recipe 2: Spicy Chicken Soup

This spicy chicken soup will warm you up from the inside.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb chicken thighs, shredded
  • 4 cups chicken broth
  • 1 can diced tomatoes
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp chili powder
  • Salt to taste

Instructions

  1. Saute onion and garlic in a pot until soft.
  2. Add shredded chicken, chicken broth, and diced tomatoes.
  3. Stir in chili powder and bring to a boil.
  4. Simmer for 15-20 minutes until flavors meld.
  5. Serve with chopped cilantro if desired.
