Recipe 1: Classic Fried Chicken

Crispy and juicy fried chicken that everyone will love.

Ingredients

  • 4 chicken drumsticks
  • 2 cups flour
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 2 cups buttermilk
  • Oil for frying

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a deep pan to 350째F (175째C).
  2. Mix flour, salt, pepper, and paprika in a bowl.
  3. Dip the chicken in buttermilk, then coat with the flour mixture.
  4. Fry for 10-12 minutes or until golden brown and fully cooked.
  5. Drain on paper towels before serving.

Recipe 2: Korean Fried Chicken

Crispy and flavorful Korean fried chicken with a sweet and spicy kick.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb chicken wings
  • 1 cup flour
  • 1/2 cup cornstarch
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/2 cup gochujang (Korean chili paste)
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce and honey
  • Oil for frying

Instructions

  1. Mix flour, cornstarch, salt, and pepper.
  2. Coat chicken wings in the flour mixture.
  3. Heat oil to 350째F (175째C) and fry wings for 8-10 minutes until crispy.
  4. In a separate pan, combine gochujang, soy sauce, and honey, then heat until warm.
  5. Toss fried wings in the sauce before serving.
